#### Kehliel

[[Kehliel\|Kehliel]] is the at the center of the [[Broadlands/Lore/Ol Tanath\|Broadlands]] and seated on the great [[Nara River\|Nara River]]. It is the old capital of the Ol-Tanath Republic. After its razing, and the death of the old King, [[Er-Lenas\|Er-Lenas]], the city hasn't been lively. However, rumor has it that the mysterious organization called the [[Broadlands/Lore/Organizations/Windless Blizzard\|Windless Blizzard]] has taken it over, restoring it to its former glory and claiming the authority associated with it.

####  Mistport

[[Mistport\|Mistport]] sits on the [[Clouded Coast\|Clouded Coast]] and is the largest trading city and port of the region. Right now, it claims authority of most of the South. It's Duke currently has the greatest claim and broadest support to ascend to the thrown of the old Republic.
