# Skydivers Guild
A guild in the city of [[Umm/Locations/Fofam\|Fofam]] where scavengers dive off the floating city into the ocean below to scavenge the [[Umm/Locations/Abyssal City\|Abyssal City]]. Guild members done special armor and weaponry to make it deep below the surface.
